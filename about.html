About

Who Are We?

We are a small family who's lives revolve around our birds. It has all started from one bird and has evolved into an unimaginable small business. Over the years we have gained a variety of birds through purposeful and accidental means. Whether its because we want or need them, or because they have been neglected or injured, we open our arms and make them one of our own.

What Do We Do?

We hand-raise birds from when they are babies, we look after peoples' birds when they are unable to do so, and we discover the gender of birds using science. We have invested in an enclosed trailer which we have set up into a lab so we are able to perform the necessary science. As well as all of these services, we are still able to maintain the wellbeing of our own birds and any additional birds that we acquire.

Why Do We Do It?

We are dedicated to our birds and want to spread our abilities to others. We want to give all birds the best opportunity possible and want to make sure that people are paired with the most suitable bird. If birds are unable to be cared for by their owners, we want to make sure that they are with people who will care for them, rather than letting them fend for themselves. Rather than having people guess a bird's gender wrong, we want to provide the owner with what it is before people assume it. These will hopefully give both the birds and the owners the best experience possible with their new lives together.
