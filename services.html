Services

We offer a range of services including: breeding, boarding and sexing, of which we offer our commitment and upmost professionalism. We dedicate our time and money to provide the highest quality possible. We are passionate about what we do and care about the experience that you and your bird have.

Breeding

We have many birds which breed annually, some of which include:

  • Alexandrians,
  • Amazons,
  • Conures,
  • Eclectuses,
  • Minature Macaws,
  • Malabars,
  • Indian Ringnecks, and
  • Plumheads.

All birds are hand-raised using specialised rearing formula. They are trained up to the point of sale and include an identification ring. They are taught how to fly which increases their positive body development and we will cut their nails and clip their wings before selling them if necessary.

Once you have decided what bird you would like contact us with what one you have chosen and we will schedule a time for you to come and meet it to make sure you are compatible with each other and that it is the one that you want to choose.

Boarding

We board a range of birds over multiple lengths of time. Whether you're going out of town for the weekend, going on holidays for a couple of weeks, or want your bird to have a vacation for a month, we have you covered. We are open to care for any bird without discrimination.

We can either use a cage that you have provided for your bird or we can use one of our spare cages. You can also supply specific food or rules for your bird or we can feed it what our birds eat and the rules that they live by. We can cut the bird's nails and clip their wings if you wish and attempt to train them if you believe that they need to be.

Just look at the calendar to see when we are already booked or when we are not available so you can decide when will best suit you and contact us about what you want.

Sexing

We discover the sex of a range of birds. Whether they are a baby and you want to know their gender to be able to name them, or they are older and you are just not able to tell, we have you covered. All it takes is a small blood sample and some science to discover what the bird's gender is.

We use PCR, a centrifugal machine and gel electrophoresis in order to analyse the DNA and discover the gender. This process can usually occur in one day allowing for quicker results sent out. There are limited companies doing this type of science in Australia so it is one of a kind.

When you have decided on what amount of samples best suits you contact us and we will give you the information on how we will send you the samples and how you can send it back to us once the birds DNA is on them.
